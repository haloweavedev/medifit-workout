[ Notes · 05 ]

Train smart.
Train safe.

01 — Form

Form first, weight second.

Always prioritize proper form over heavier loads. Quality movement prevents injury and builds the right pattern.

02 — Hydrate

Drink before, during, after.

Hydration supports performance and recovery. Don't wait until you're thirsty — you're already late.

03 — Rest

Honor the rest periods.

Rest is part of the program. Cutting it short reduces strength gains. Set a timer, breathe, reset.

04 — Track

Write it down.

Note weights, reps, and how you felt. Progress is a series of small wins you'd otherwise forget.
